Features

Everything your household needs

Built around real pain points discovered through user research with students, working professionals, and homemakers.

📦

Shared inventory

One centralized grocery list accessible by all household members with real-time updates.

🔔

Expiry alerts

Color-coded status: red = expiring, yellow = frequently bought, green = fresh. Never miss a spoil date.

💸

Expense splitting

Track who paid for what and split grocery costs fairly among all members automatically.

🛒

Smart shopping list

Auto-generated based on current stock and consumption patterns to avoid over-purchasing.

🏷️

Categories & tags

Organize items by vegetables, dairy, grains, snacks — with quantity and unit tracking.

📊

Spending insights

View daily, weekly, and monthly grocery spending summaries. Know where your money goes.
