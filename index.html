Features

Everything your household needs

Built around real pain points discovered through user research with students, working professionals, and homemakers.

Shared inventory

One centralized grocery list accessible by all household members with real-time updates.

Expiry alerts

Color-coded status: red = low stock, yellow = expiring, green = fresh. Never miss a spoil date.
